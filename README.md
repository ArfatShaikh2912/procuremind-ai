## 📌 Problem Statement

Enterprise procurement processes are vulnerable to vendor
fraud, contract mismanagement, and poor risk assessment.
Manual reviews are slow, inconsistent, and fail to scale
across hundreds of procurement records.

---

## 💡 Solution

ProcureMind AI is an intelligent procurement decision engine
that automatically scores vendor risk, detects fraud patterns,
and provides explainable AI reasoning for every decision —
giving procurement teams full transparency and control.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🔍 **Vendor Risk Scoring** — trust, risk, and fraud scores
- 📄 **Contract Analysis** — automated contract red-flag detection
- 🚨 **Fraud Detection** — ML-based anomaly detection engine
- 🤖 **Explainable AI** — full reasoning chains for every decision
- 📊 **Executive Dashboard** — real-time procurement intelligence
- 📦 **290+ Records Analyzed** — enterprise-scale testing

---

## 🏗️ Architecture
Data Input (290+ Procurement Records)
↓
Python ML Backend
↓
Explainable AI Engine (Trust + Risk + Fraud Scores)
↓
Streamlit Executive Dashboard
